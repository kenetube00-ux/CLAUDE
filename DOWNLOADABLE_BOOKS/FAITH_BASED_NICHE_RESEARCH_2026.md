**FULL CONTENT OUTLINE — Book #2: Scripture-Based CBT Anxiety Workbook**

**Title:** "Peace Beyond Understanding: A Scripture-Based CBT Workbook for Christian Women with Anxiety"

**Subtitle:** "8-Week Evidence-Based Program Integrating Cognitive Behavioral Therapy with Biblical Truth for Lasting Peace"

**Structure:** 130-150 pages, 8.5x11

**SECTION 1: UNDERSTANDING (Weeks 1-2)**

Week 1: Understanding Anxiety Through Faith & Science
- What anxiety is (biological + spiritual perspective)
- "Is it a sin to be anxious?" — Addressing the guilt (Philippians 4:6-7 in context)
- How God designed your nervous system (fearfully and wonderfully made)
- Your anxiety profile (self-assessment: physical/cognitive/behavioral)
- Scripture: Psalm 34:4, 1 Peter 5:7, Isaiah 41:10
- Exercise: Anxiety symptom tracker (7-day baseline)

Week 2: The CBT-Faith Connection — How Thoughts Shape Your Peace
- The CBT triangle: Thoughts → Feelings → Behaviors
- Biblical cognitive restructuring: "taking every thought captive" (2 Corinthians 10:5)
- Identifying automatic negative thoughts (ANTs)
- The difference between worry and prayer (Matthew 6:25-34)
- Exercise: Thought record introduction (with scripture column)

**SECTION 2: CHALLENGING (Weeks 3-5)**

Week 3: Cognitive Distortions & Lies of the Enemy
- 10 common cognitive distortions (catastrophizing, all-or-nothing, etc.)
- Biblical truth for each distortion (matching scripture to each pattern)
- "The truth will set you free" applied to thought patterns (John 8:32)
- Exercise: Distortion identification worksheets (5 pages)
- Scripture meditation: Philippians 4:8 (think on these things)

Week 4: Building Your Biblical Thought Record
- 7-column thought record adapted for faith integration:
  Situation | Emotion | Automatic Thought | Cognitive Distortion | Evidence For/Against | Balanced Thought | Biblical Truth
- Worked examples from women's lives (motherhood, marriage, work, ministry)
- Exercise: 5 completed thought records with scaffolding
- Scripture: Romans 12:2 (renewing your mind)

Week 5: Behavioral Experiments — Acting in Faith
- What behavioral experiments are (testing anxious predictions)
- "Walking on water" — Peter's experiment in faith (Matthew 14:28-31)
- Planning experiments for common anxiety scenarios
- Exercise: Experiment planner (prediction/experiment/actual outcome/what I learned)
- Scripture: Joshua 1:9 (be strong and courageous)

**SECTION 3: PRACTICING (Weeks 6-8)**

Week 6: Exposure & Trust — Facing Fears with God
- Gradual exposure explained (the opposite of avoidance)
- Fear hierarchy builder (from least to most anxiety-provoking)
- "When I am afraid, I will trust in You" — Psalm 56:3
- Safety behaviors vs. faith-based coping
- Exercise: Exposure hierarchy worksheet + tracking log
- Scripture: Isaiah 43:1-3 (when you pass through the waters)

Week 7: Body & Spirit — Physical Tools for Anxious Moments
- Diaphragmatic breathing as prayer ("Be still and know" — Psalm 46:10)
- Progressive muscle relaxation with scripture meditation
- Grounding in God's presence (5-4-3-2-1 + gratitude)
- Exercise: Daily relaxation practice log
- Scripture: Psalm 131:2 (like a weaned child is my soul within me)

Week 8: Maintaining Peace — Relapse Prevention & Spiritual Disciplines
- Building a sustainable peace practice
- Spiritual disciplines that support mental health (prayer, community, rest, worship)
- Warning signs and action plan
- When to seek professional help (therapy is not failure)
- Exercise: My personalized peace plan
- Scripture: Philippians 4:6-7 (do not be anxious about anything)

**BONUS SECTIONS:**
- For husbands/partners: How to support her (3-page guide)
- For church leaders: Recommended mental health resources
- Weekly prayer prompts (8 weeks)
- Scripture memory cards for anxiety (printable/perforated)
- Weekly mood tracker (8 pages)
- "My anxiety story" — narrative processing page
