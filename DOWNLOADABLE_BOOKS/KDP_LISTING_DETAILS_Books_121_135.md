### DESCRIPTION (4,000 characters max):
```
Are intrusive thoughts controlling your life? Do you feel trapped in repetitive rituals you can't stop? This evidence-based OCD Therapy Workbook gives you the tools to fight back.

Based on Exposure and Response Prevention (ERP) — the gold-standard treatment for OCD recommended by therapists worldwide — this workbook guides you step-by-step through breaking the OCD cycle.

WHAT'S INSIDE:

★ Understanding Your OCD — Learn how the OCD cycle works (trigger → obsession → anxiety → compulsion → temporary relief → repeat) and why your rituals actually make OCD stronger.

★ ERP Hierarchy Builder — Create your personalized fear ladder, ranking situations from least to most anxiety-provoking, so you can face fears gradually and safely.

★ 10 Exposure Practice Log Pages — Track every exposure exercise with before/during/after anxiety ratings (SUDS scale), rituals resisted, and progress notes.

★ Cognitive Restructuring Exercises — Challenge the distorted thinking patterns that fuel your OCD, including overestimation of threat, intolerance of uncertainty, and magical thinking.

★ Types of OCD Covered — Contamination, harm/violence, symmetry/ordering, religious/scrupulosity, relationship OCD, and pure-O (purely obsessional).

★ 12-Week ERP Schedule — A structured timeline to guide your recovery journey from week 1 through graduation.

★ Relapse Prevention Plan — Identify your warning signs and create a concrete action plan for maintaining your progress long-term.

WHO THIS WORKBOOK IS FOR:
• Adults currently in therapy who need structured homework exercises
• Individuals waiting for therapy who want to begin self-help immediately
• Therapists looking for client worksheets and between-session assignments
• Anyone diagnosed with OCD seeking evidence-based tools for recovery

This is NOT a replacement for professional treatment. It IS the most comprehensive self-guided ERP workbook available — designed to complement therapy or serve as a structured starting point for your recovery journey.

WORKBOOK FEATURES:
• 8.5 x 11 inch format (large, easy-to-write-in pages)
• 120+ pages of guided exercises and worksheets
• Matte paperback cover for durability
• Black & white interior (printer-friendly)
• Can be used alongside any OCD treatment program

Take the first step toward freedom from OCD. Your recovery starts with one brave exposure at a time.
```
